Ping Pong Cavite

This website provides services for table tennis players in Cavite, hence the name.

Site Purpose

Allows visitors to discover events, products, and register their email to receive news and updates.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming table tennis events?
  • Where can I buy this type of rubber?
  • How do I get updated?

Typography

  • Lato: content
